wanna let you know that you are not alone, here are some true shared stories

Shona

I had social anxiety, for example when trying to do a presentation in front of a group or talking to strangers. I've learned to recognize unreasonable thoughts that make me afraid and argue against them. I'm much better now.

Raya

My anxiety was caused by a ton of different factors: cPTSD from abusive childhood, specific phobias/triggers due to car accident and sexual assault, hormonal disorders causing anxiety/depression as symptoms, being in a bad financial/relationship situation and bad health problems causing pain/stress/hopelessness.

Lottie

Used to be terrified of leaving the house, of getting ill, etc. I was especially scared of things like getting lost in a car with my dad or either of my parents dying. after working with family and friends and following some the anti-anxiety techniques online, I was talking to my parents the other day about going on a road trip with my dad to see my auntie, and I thought to myself oh my god, I've made it. I've conquered my fears. The fears and anxiety are still there, but nowhere near as bad

Shanice

I've had anxiety all my life, and it gets pretty damn bad sometimes. My latest anxiety/depression cycle had me deep in the muck for two years (2013-2015). In the beginning/worst of it, I found myself missing work because of anxiety attacks and crying fits that would last hours. It hurt my relationship (which was the major reason I was anxious anyway) and kept me from enjoying much of my life.
